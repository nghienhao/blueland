Makes Stylish Entrance in Spring-Summer-2022 Collection with Wang Yi Bo

The SS22 Men’s Collection of Japanese high fashion brand L&S have now hit stores, presenting another novel takes on the luxurious Spring/Summer wardrobe. This season comes with a dynamic ensemble of styles, where creative ingenuity shines through each and every detail to push the styling envelope with a mythical spin. Wintry materials and hues come together for cocooning cosiness, unveiling an unexpectedly mellow facet of the street dandy. This Spring, wrap yourself up in L&S and hit the streets with global ambassador Wang Yi Bo!

In line with L&S’s spirit of quality excellence, corduroy denim and washed jacquard denim make luxurious entrance for an ever more collectible collection. Denim fabrics in different textures are patched together, dotted with rivets, metal buckle and multi-pockets for a perfect union of workwear references and denim items. Retro checkered blazer and corduroy jacket are the seasonal must-haves for a vintage ride in a cosy winter season.
